Welcome to our group website!

IIE-NIP group is affiliated with the No.3 Research Laboratory of Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and was established in 2019. The team mainly focuses on academic research and engineering contribution in the field of NLP. In recent years, the team made a lot of achievements in the directions of sentiment analysis, machine reading comprehension, model compression etc. Additionally, the team won the First Prize in “Military Intelligence· Machine Comprehension” contest in 2018 and 2019 consecutively.

At present, there are 2 experienced instructors、 4 doctoral students and 4 master students in the team. Zheng Lin, as the team leader, received her PhD degree in Institute of Computing Technology, CAS in 2014. She is currently an associate professor and acted as a doctoral supervisor of Institute of Information Engineering，CAS. In the past few years, she had published nearly 50 papers on top conferences of AI and NLP, including ACL, EMNLP, AAAI, IJCAI, WWW, CIKM, WSDM, ICMR etc. In addition to that, she also shared her understanding of the theories and methods in NLP and published an academic monograph 《Text Sentiment Analysis》 in 2019.

中国科学院信息工程研究所第三研究室NLP小组成立于2019年，主要致力于自然语言处理领域的学术研究和应用实践，具体包括情感/情绪分析、机器阅读理解、神经网络模型压缩等研究任务。在2018、2019年，连续获得两届全国“军事智能-机器阅读”挑战赛冠军。目前，小组共有2位指导老师、4名在读博士研究生、4名在读硕士研究生。

林政老师目前是三室NLP小组的负责人，她于2014年在中国科学院计算技术研究所获得博士学位，目前是中科院信工所的副研究员、博士生导师。过去几年，她在人工智能、自然语言处理等领域发表论文近50篇，包括 ACL, EMNLP, AAAI, IJCAI, WWW, CIKM, WSDM, ICMR等等，出版了一部学术专著《文本情感分析》。此外，她还担任了CCF大数据专家委员会通讯委员、ACL、EMNLP、AAAI、IEEE/ACM Transactions on Audio Speech and Language Processing、计算机学报、中文信息学报等多个会议和期刊的审稿人。
