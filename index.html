IIE-NLP

IIE-NLP group focuses on academic research and engineering contribution in NLP. In recent years, the team made a lot of achievements in the directions of sentiment analysis, machine reading comprehension, model compression etc.
At present, there are 2 experienced instructors, 8 PhD candidates and 4 master students in the team. Prof. Zheng Lin, as the team leader, received her PhD degree in Institute of Computing Technology, CAS in 2014. In the past few years, she had published over 50 papers on top conferences of AI and NLP, including ACL, EMNLP, AAAI, IJCAI, WWW, CIKM, WSDM, ICMR, etc.
To apply for graduate studies or an internship, please submit your resume to: linzheng@iie.ac.cn
